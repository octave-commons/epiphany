```clojure
(κ decision-candidate authority evidence alternatives consequences
   -> accepted-decision)

(α target criteria evidence reviewer authority

```

They are declared as valid design data before all workflow, record, checker, or automation support exists.

## Important constraints

- **Decision policy:** A design recommendation, merge, or conversation does not become an accepted decision without named authority and acceptance.
- **Review policy:** A green check records a procedure result; it is not acceptance by itself. Failures and unavailable environments remain first-class results.
- **Engineering policy:** A feature is not demonstrated merely because it compiles, has a stub, or succeeds on a happy-path demo. The evidence must identify the contract/mode/boundary actually checked.
