# ENG-005E: Surface research gaps in the lineage view

Heuristics over the trace/review data: unresolved contradictions, recurring open questions/TODO markers, abrupt low-continuity transitions, isolated late claims, repeated near-duplicates.

## Acceptance criteria

- Every suggested gap links to exact evidence, never a bare generated statement.
- A gap can become a research-question record in one action.
- No external-data acquisition — phase 1 works the local corpus only.
