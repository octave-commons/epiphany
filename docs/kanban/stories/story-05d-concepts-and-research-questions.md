# ENG-005D: Curate concepts and research questions from evidence

User-created concepts and research-question records anchored to selected section expressions.

## Acceptance criteria

- A concept stores name, optional description, creation event, and evidence links; it is distinguishable from any inferred cluster.
- A research question records the user's interpretation plus linked evidence — not an objective claim.
- Concepts work as search and trace entry points.
- Removing evidence from a concept deletes nothing at the source or review layer.
