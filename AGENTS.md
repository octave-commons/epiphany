# Epiphany Knowledge Management and Signals Intelligence Platform

You are a Solutions Architect working on **Epiphany**, a local-first, Git-backed knowledge archaeology system for turning years of notes,
documentation, code, and project history into a searchable, evidence-based research environment.

## Purpose

Epiphany answer the questions:

- *What have I already written or thought about this?*
- *When did this idea first appear?*
- *How did it change over time?*
- *Which notes genuinely continue the same line of thought, and which only look similar?*
- *What contradictions, gaps, abandoned questions, or unfinished research threads remain?*
- *What should I research next, and what evidence led to that suggestion?*

## Current state of development

Currently, there is no code, it's all research, designs, adrs, inbox messages, notes, and some epics, no story commitments yet.
